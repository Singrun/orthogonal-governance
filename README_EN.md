## Core Concepts

- **Collinearity Trap**: When audit and business share the same coordinate system, oversight becomes complicity, leading to overlapping blind spots.
- **CSI (Collusion Susceptibility Index)**: Measures the distance from "Collusion Equilibrium," predicting the probability of systemic corruption.
- **Orthogonal Governance**: Preventing the system from sliding into thermodynamic equilibrium (death) by introducing independent observation and damping mechanisms.
- **Tier-2 Block**: The phenomenon where information flows in informal networks but is truncated in formal feedback loops.
- **High-Entropy Order**: A critical state where rules are orderly and data is perfect, but information is distorted and innovation stagnates.
